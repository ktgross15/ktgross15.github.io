About

Hello! My name is Katie, I'm a Brooklyn-based queer data science manager with an insatiable curiosity. I currently lead a team of Data Scientists at a data science software company, Dataiku, and mentor aspiring data scientists on the side.

In my spare time, I enjoy yoga, rock climbing, comedy shows, discovering new music, and learning about and investing in the crypto space.

Work

I lead a team of Data Scientists at Dataiku, a leading AI software company that develops a collaborative end-to-end data science platform (DSS). My team focuses on enabling our customers to integrate AI into their businesses through coaching and co-development of machine learning projects using DSS.

To learn a little bit more about my role, but primarily the snacks in Dataiku's office, feel free to check out this Day in the Life Video.

Outside of my day-to-day role, my colleague Hadrien and I have co-founded Dataiku's queer ERG which now has over 50 members of LGBTQIA+ identifying employees and allies.

Teaching

I've always been passionate about teaching and sharing knowledge. I spent many years in and after college volunteering to build SAT prep programs for students in various underserved communities throughout New York and Connecticut, through Let's Get Ready and New York Cares.

Today, to help others build careers in data science, I work as a mentor for a program called Sharpest Minds.

I've also developed a lot of educational content for Dataiku. Below is a collection of popular content I've created or participated in, focused on teaching AI/ML basics:

  • ML Basics and Linear Models: In Plain English
  • Tree-Based Models: In Plain English
  • Clustering: In Plain English
  • Dimensionality Reduction: In Plain English
  • Recommendation Engines: In Plain English
  • Natural Language Processing: In Plain English
  • Key Applications of ML in Fashion
  • Webinar: ML Basics
  • Webinar: Getting Started with NLP
  • Ask the Experts: 2020 Data Science Trends Report

Contact
